Cornel West 2024 - Ballot Access Launch HQ

With the launch of our Love Warrior volunteer program, we have officially begun our 50 State push for Ballot Access. We are on the Ballot in Alaska, Oregon, South Carolina, and Utah -- and we need your help in the rest of the country!


Please step up and answer the call to become an Elector, and represent Brother West for the Presidential Electoral College; or take the lead on our petitioning operations, either hosting Love Warrior events, or at your own pace. Sign up now!

Petitioning Leads - Lead events to gather signatures, assist other volunteers on the ground, and coordinate with the campaign
Electors - Sign your name to documents and vote for Brother West in the Electoral College
